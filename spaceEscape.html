Space Escape

Try your luck navigating the Rocketship out of this cavern to safety.
Complete with 9 Levels of varying difficulty to test your skills and your patience.

Do you have what it takes to get out safely?

How to Play:

- Use the 'Left' and 'Right' arrow keys to steer the Rocketship.
- Press the 'SPACE' bar to thrust the rocket.

Objective:

- Navigate the Rocketship to the Green landing platform.
- Be careful to avoid obstacles, the ground, and the roof of the cave.

Space Escape

This Rocketship was navigating a cave system on a distant planet, when it gets lost. Now it is up to you take the controls and fly your Rocketship to safety.

Space Escape is one of the very first games I built and shared when being introduced to Unity.
Building this game gave me a chance to introduce particle systems, in game physics, animations and more. Future builds will include mobile controls and UI for Android and iOS.

Even as an early project I enjoy the challenge and fun simplicity that Space Escape provides, and it provides a great benchmark to look back on.

How To Play

  • Move:
    Use the 'Left' and 'Right' arrow keys to steer the Rocketship left or right.

  • Thrust:
    Use the 'SPACE' bar to provide thrust to the Rocketship.

    • Objective

  • Escape
    Navigate the Rocketship safely to the Green landing platform to advance to the next part of the cave.
