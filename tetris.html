Tetris 2D Clone

This remixed clone is my version of a fully functional Tetris clone, with a line counter and progressively faster difficulty. Complete with a pause/restart menu, music and sound effects, and the ability to hold a piece off to the side and call it back, this clone is an updated, refreshing remix of the 80's classic.

Building this game gave me a chance to explore particle systems, immersive audio, and animations more in depth. Future builds will include mobile controls and UI for Android and iOS.

I chose to make a Tetris Clone because growing up I remember watching my Nana play Tetris on her GameBoy every night before bed. She would call me and my brother over each time she had beaten the game to watch the rocket ship blast off!
These early memories of my Nana are some of my favorite, and if she were here I know she would love playing this game that I made this game for her.

How To Play

  • Move:
    Use the 'Arrow' keys to move a game piece left, right, or down.

  • Rotate:
    Use the 'Up' Arrow to rotate a game piece.

  • Speed Up:
    Use the 'Down' arrow to speed up the falling piece.

  • Change Rotation:
    Use the '/' to change to the rotation direction clockwise or counter-clockwise, or hit the on screen button.

  • Hold a Piece:
    Use the 'Right Shift' to hold a game piece off to the side, or to recall that piece.

  • Pause:
    Use the 'Escape' key to pause the game, or hit the on screen button.
