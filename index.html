Who Am I?

Brandyn Reindel:
-Software Engineer
-Unity Developer
-All Around Nice Guy!

I am a Full-Stack Software Engineer. I attended Holberton School in New Haven, CT, and completed a second year specialization in AR/VR and Unity Development (Graduated January 2021). In March of 2020 I completed the rigorous, fast-paced foundations curriculum at Holberton School. Where I was taught not just what to learn, but how to learn, through peer learning and a full-stack project-based curriculum. Holberton has given me the skills to not only be a great software engineer, but a great team member.

Born and raised in CT, and currently living in Hamden. I am a fan all things Tech, Augmented and Virtual Reality, and all aspects of Software and Game Development.

DuckHunt2020:

An Augmented Adeventure

For my AR/VR Specialization portfolio project I intended to create a new, modern version of the 1984 Nintendo classic Duck Hunt. This game was designed to be more immersive and interactive than any other game on the market at the time. And it is a game, that in my opinion, was far ahead of its time. However Duck Hunt has never gained the same success in later releases, as other titles in the Nintendo family. So I built a 21st Century version, that is relevant with today's available technology.
