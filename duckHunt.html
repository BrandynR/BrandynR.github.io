DuckHunt 2020: An Augmented Adventure

For my AR/VR Specialization portfolio project I created a new, modern version, of the 1984 Nintendo classic Duck Hunt.
Duck Hunt was designed to be more immersive and interactive than any other game on the market at the time. And it is a game, that in my opinion, was far ahead of its time.
However Duck Hunt has never gained the same success in later releases, as other titles in the Nintendo family.
So I built a 21st Century version, that is relevant with today's available technology.

Built in Unity and utilizing Vuforia's API, DuckHunt 2020 combined aspects from every part of my Holberton AR/VR Specialization curriculum.

How To Play

  • Install:
    Download the APK here, to install on your device.

  • Print:
    Print off a copy of the 'Image Target' to be tracked.

  • Shoot!
    With your device in hand, move around the Image Target to find the ducks flying around.
    Tap the Bullseye in the lower right to fire.
